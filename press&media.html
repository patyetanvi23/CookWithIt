About the Project

Smart Recipe Finder is a web platform designed to help users cook smarter, not harder. By entering the ingredients you already have at home, the app generates delicious and practical recipe ideas. It focuses on simplicity, responsive design, and food-centric features.

Key Features

Search & Filter

Find recipes quickly by filtering ingredients or cuisine types.

Recipe Cards

Beautiful recipe cards with pop-ups for details and instructions.

Favorites

Save your favorite dishes locally to revisit anytime.

Responsive Design

Works smoothly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
