Accessibility Statement

Ensuring Smart Recipe Finder is usable for everyone

Our Commitment

At Smart Recipe Finder, we are committed to making our platform accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. Our goal is to provide a smooth, inclusive cooking discovery experience for everyone.

Accessibility Features

  • Responsive design that works across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
  • High-contrast colors and readable fonts for better visibility.
  • Alternative text descriptions for images and recipe cards.
  • Keyboard navigation support for searching and browsing recipes.
  • Clear labels for search inputs, buttons, and pop-up recipe cards.

Ongoing Improvements

We are continually working to improve the accessibility of Smart Recipe Finder. During our hackathon development phase, we test features to ensure smoother interaction for all users.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using Smart Recipe Finder, please let us know. Your feedback helps us improve.
